01 — The Challenge

A Structural Gap in Global Distribution

Global fashion supply operates through independent boutiques, platforms and brand ecosystems. China functions under a distinct regulatory, logistical and commercial framework. Between these systems lies a structural gap.

Regulatory Complexity

China's distinct regulatory framework creates barriers to entry that unstructured approaches cannot navigate effectively.

Logistical Fragmentation

Disconnected systems between global supply and Chinese distribution create inefficiencies and delays.

Commercial Barriers

Different commercial frameworks require specialized knowledge and established relationships to operate successfully.

Unstructured access creates friction. Structured access creates scalability.
