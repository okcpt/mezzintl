Our History

A decade of bridging global fashion with Chinese consumers

October 2012
Launched our first online store on Taobao, beginning the journey of bringing global fashion to China.
December 2015
Surpassed 10,000 customers, achieving annual sales revenue of over 25 million RMB.
January 2018
Opened flagship store on JD.com and launched Mezzanine WeChat Mini Program.
December 2018
Reached 50,000 customers, with annual sales exceeding 80 million RMB.
June 2019
Launched the Mezzanine App, expanding our direct-to-consumer offering.
December 2023
Exceeded 300,000 registered users, with annual sales surpassing 280 million RMB.
February 2024
Received China Customs AEO certification, enhancing cross-border trade efficiency.
May 2025
Officially launched the ALFAMODA App, focused on developing and serving the fashion wholesale market.
