01 — The Challenge

A Structural Gap

Global Supply
China Market

Global fashion supply is fragmented across continents, systems, and standards. Each region operates with its own logistics frameworks, compliance requirements, and commercial protocols.

China operates a distinct ecosystem—unique regulatory environments, platform architectures, and consumer behaviors that differ fundamentally from Western markets.

Between these two systems lies a structural gap: the absence of a coherent, compliant, and scalable bridge that allows global supply to flow efficiently into China's marketplace.

02 — The Solution

A Distribution Layer

Mezzanine operates as a structured distribution layer—an intermediary infrastructure that translates, connects, and optimizes the flow of global fashion into China.
