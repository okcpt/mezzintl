03 — The Platform

Integrated Infrastructure

Our network spans 150+ supply partners, representing 5,000+ brands and over 4 million SKUs across global fashion categories.

Supply enters China through a compliant cross-border framework, supporting both B2C and B2B distribution.

Technology synchronizes inventory, pricing and orders within a unified operational layer.
